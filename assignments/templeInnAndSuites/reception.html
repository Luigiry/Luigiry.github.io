Reception Services

Each of our Inns has a reception hall that can be reserved to wedding receptions or other special occations. This reception has access to a kitchen that can be used for food prepearation and serving. We also have a simple catering menu you can take advantage of.

Reception Hall

When you reserve our reception hall we will provide table, table cloths, chairs, dishes, silverware, and some basic decorations. If you wish to decorate the hall yourselves our staff will be happy to help you. Our reception halls have build in sound systems and plenty of room for dancing. There will be staff on hand to help serve food and ensure that you enjoy reception, and don't worry about clean up our staff has it covered.

Catering

When you choose to have us cater your event you can pick 2 main courses, 2 sides, and a dessert. Additional selections are available for an addtional charge.
