Seattle Washington

The Seattle Washington temple is dedicated November 17th 1980. The temple is located on a gente hill just off I-90. This temple was the last temple dedicated by President Spencer W. Kimball and is only 1 of 5 temples where the angel Moroni statue is holding the gold plates. The Seattle Washington temple is the 19th operating temple for The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-Day Saints.
